### **Intro**

**Theorem Statement**

> $X, Y$ is a normed space, and $V$ is a compact subset of the $X$ and $f$ is a continuous mapping, then $f(V)$  is also a compact subset of $Y$. 

**Source:**

Theorem 2.4 in the Friendly Intro Textbook.
