### **Minty's Theorem**

> Let $A$ be a multi-valued mapping on some Hilbert space: $\mathcal H$, and $A$ is monotone. Then $A$ is *maximal monotone* if and only if $\text{ran}(I + A) = \mathcal H$ (The operator is surjective).
