Introduction

Given a vector xxx in Cn\mathbb{C}^nCn, and we want to transform the vector onto another vector say sss using the hyperplane seperating betweeen sss, vvv while at the same time producing the matrix for such a transformation.

In general, we are looking for the matrix which performs a reflection transformation on vectors in Cn\mathbb{C}^nCn where, and the plane of reflection is the hyperplane seperating the vector xxx and yyy where these 2 vectors are not collinear and the vector yyy is also unitary (complex modulus is 1)

Let vector v=y−xv = y - xv=y−x. Then, the hyperplane that is separating these 2 vector xxx, yyy will be orthogonal to vvv.

Firstly, perform a projection of vvv onto the orthogonal subspace hyperplane, which is the vector vvv; which is given by:
