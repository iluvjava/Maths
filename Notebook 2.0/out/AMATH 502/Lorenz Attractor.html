And, there are 2 sub-critical limit cycles around 2 of the fixed points as rrr goes slightly above 111.

And when the limit cycles in 3D collapse onto the fixed points, it becomes the Lorenz strange attractor.

The lorenz attractor only happens when the value of rrr is on the right range.
