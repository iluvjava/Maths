Fundamental Theorem of Linear Programming

  • No optimal --> infeasible or unbounded
  • Feasible exists --> There is a basis of vector that achieve feasibility
  • There is optimal --> At least one optimal exists

Optimal Basis

  • Chose of a set of columns in AAA such that, it fixes a solution for corresponding xxx vector for the system Ax=bAx = bAx=b, among all such a collection of columns, find the basis such that, after solving xxx on the partial matrix, we obtain maximal value for cTxc^TxcTx
  • The rank of the optimal basis is mmm.
