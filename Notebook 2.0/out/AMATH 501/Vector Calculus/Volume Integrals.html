Curvilinear Coordinates

Under the curvilinear coordinates, the integral will be redefined. Suppoed that we are looking the curilinear mapping r(u1,u2,u3)=(u1(u1,u2,u3),u2(u1,u2,u3),u3(u1,u2,u3))r(u_1, u_2, u_3) = (u_1(u_1, u_2, u_3), u_2(u_1, u_2, u_3), u_3(u_1, u_2, u_3))r(u1​,u2​,u3​)=(u1​(u1​,u2​,u3​),u2​(u1​,u2​,u3​),u3​(u1​,u2​,u3​)).
