### **Intro**

Moments of Samples: 

$$
\frac{1}{n}\sum_{i = 1}^{n}x_i
$$

k th Moments for a Random Variable: 

$$
\mathbb{E}[X^{k}]
$$

kth Moment of $X$ about constant $c$ is: 

$$
\mathbb{E}[(x - c)^k]
$$
